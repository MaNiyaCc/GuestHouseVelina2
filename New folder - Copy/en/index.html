House Description

Guest house Velina is an old town house in the seaside village Velika. The house is completely renovated, ready to welcome guests from all around the world. The three stores house has 4 bedrooms, living room, kitchen, 3 bathrooms,small yard and a summer kitchen. It is perfect for big groups or families. The nearest beach is within 5 minutes drive, in village Lozenetz. There you can also find supermarkets, greens market, bars and restaurants.The house is surrounded by beautiful forrest, perfect for walks, when the heat is unbearable.
